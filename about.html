About Mikey's

Michael McGehee, owner and founder of Mikey's Card Shop, started collecting Pokemon cards at a very young age. As an adult he now wants to bring the same excitement he felt as a child to all other collectors of Pokemon cards. He sells cards from all packs and all years to collectors new and old.
